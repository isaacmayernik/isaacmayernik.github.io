About me

I grew up in Greenville, South Carolina. I currently attend the University of South Carolina, with plans to graduate in the Spring of 2025 with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. I am currently designing a mobile application with a team as a capstone project.

I love to workout, play video games, go skiing and kayaking, watch soccer, and be with family and friends.

I am driven to make games due to my passion for playing them, and am heavily inspired by many such as Dark Souls, Super Metroid, and Bioshock. I hope to develop video games that many will enjoy.

Workout App

Still in development at the University of South Carolina in CSCE546. The Github repository may become private. An Android app that utilizes drawn vectors to update muscle groups based on which workouts a user adds to their daily workout log.

CUHackit25

Participated in the Clemson University 2025 Hackathon (CUHackit25). Developed an Android app with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Python, and Python Flask that utilizes the AWS services provided during the event to create a prompt based on user entered information and generate a workout plan based on it.

Forgotten

Forgotten is a 3D Souls-like action-adventure game developed in UE5. The player controls a fox who must survive against enemies, defeat the golem boss, and reach the rest point.

Frogbound

Frogbound is a 2D Metroidvania game developed in Unity. The player controls a frog as they explore levels, unlock upgrades, and defeat enemies.

Pocket Campus

Developed for a Software Engineering course, Pocket Campus was designed to be a system that would allow students to see which classes they still needed to complete their degree.

Senior Project

Senior project for Brashier Middle College. Learned the basics of Unreal Engine 4, the blueprint system, and game design.
