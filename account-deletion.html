How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Liftly account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. We're committed to protecting your privacy and will process your request promptly.

1

Send an email to our support team using the following details:

Email Address: workoutappbusiness@gmail.com

Subject: Liftly Account Deletion Request

2

In your email, please include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Liftly account
  • Your username (if different from your email)
  • Any additional information that will help us identify your account
3

We will send a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted. This process typically takes 3-5 business days.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your workout data, progress history, and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.

If you have any questions about this process, please don't hesitate to contact us at the same email address.
