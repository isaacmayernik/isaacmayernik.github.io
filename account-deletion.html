Request Account Deletion

Permanently delete your Liftly account and all associated data.

⚠️ This action is permanent

Your account, profile, workout history, friends, and all personal data will be deleted from our servers. This cannot be undone.

What will be deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials (cannot log in again)
  • All workout history and logs
  • Personal records and achievements
  • All saved routines
  • Goals and progress tracking
  • Profile information (name, username, email)
  • Profile photo
  • Friend connections and friend requests
  • Reactions sent and received
  • Muscle color history
  • Theme and app settings
  • Notification preferences

What happens to your local data:

  • Your workouts are saved locally on your device
  • You are automatically converted to a guest user
  • Your data stays on your device but is removed from our servers
💡 Your workouts will NOT be lost. They will be saved locally on your device. You can continue using Liftly as a guest, or create a new account later. Your profile, friends, and social data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Or delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open Liftly on your device
  2. Go to Profile Tab → Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to Other section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion — your account will be removed from our servers

Note: Your workouts will be saved locally as a guest. You can sign up again later with a new account.
