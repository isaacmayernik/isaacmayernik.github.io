Request Data Deletion

Request to delete your personal data from Liftly's servers.

What happens when you request data deletion?

We will delete your workout history, personal information, and all associated data from our servers. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law.

Data that will be deleted:

  • All workout history and logs
  • Personal records and achievements
  • All saved routines
  • Goals and progress tracking
  • Muscle color history
  • Theme and app settings

Data that will be kept:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile information (name, username, email)
  • Profile photo
  • Friends list and connections
  • Reactions sent and received
  • Notification preferences
⚠️ This action only deletes your workout data, not your account. Your profile and social connections remain intact. You can continue logging new workouts immediately.

Or delete data directly from the app:

  1. Open Liftly on your device
  2. Go to Profile Tab → Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to Other section
  4. Tap Reset All Data to delete all workouts, goals, and settings

Note: "Reset All Data" keeps your account but clears local data.
"Delete Account" permanently removes your account and all data from our servers.
