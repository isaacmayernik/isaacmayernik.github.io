Liftly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Liftly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness tracking application.

By using Liftly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, first and last name, and username.
  • Profile Data: You may choose to add a profile photo to your account.

Fitness and Health Information

  • Workout Data: We collect information about your workouts, including exercises performed, sets, reps, weights, cardio sessions, duration, distance, and calories burned.
  • Fitness Goals: Your personal fitness goals and targets.
  • Progress Tracking: Personal records and workout history.

Technical Information

  • App Usage: Information about how you interact with our app.
  • Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostic information to help us improve app performance.
  • Device Information: Basic device information and app version data.

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide and maintain our fitness tracking services, including workout logging, progress tracking, and social features.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account.
  • Social Features: To enable friend connections and allow users to view each other's workout data (when mutually connected).
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and improve our services.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent activity and ensure app security.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide support.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

With Other Users

When you connect with other users as friends, they can see:

  • Your username and profile information
  • Your workout data and progress

With Service Providers

We use third-party services that help us operate our app:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • Expo: For app development and performance monitoring

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using secure protocols
  • We implement access controls and authentication measures
  • We regularly review our security practices

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update your personal information through the app settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Social Controls: You can manage your friend connections and block other users.

Account Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please email us at:

workoutappbusiness@gmail.com

Include your account email and username in your request. We will process deletion requests within 3-5 business days.

You can also visit our Account Deletion Page for more information.

Children's Privacy

Liftly is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
